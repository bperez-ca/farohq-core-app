# FARO SMB Pricing & Features Guide

## Overview
This document outlines the pricing tiers and features available to Small and Medium-sized Businesses (SMBs) through FARO's white-label Local Visibility platform. SMBs access FARO through their marketing agency partner's branded portal.

## Key Features Explained

### Unified Inbox
- **WhatsApp Business**: Primary communication channel in LATAM (conversation limits per tier)
- **Instagram DMs**: Respond to Instagram direct messages (Plus and above)
- **Facebook Messenger**: Handle Facebook messages (Plus and above)
- **Google Business Messages**: Respond to Google My Business inquiries
- **Email**: Unified email management (Plus and above)
- **Web Chat Widget**: Website chat integration (Plus and above)

### Reviews Management
- **Monitor**: Aggregate reviews from Google, Facebook, and other platforms
- **Respond**: Reply to reviews directly from FARO
- **AI Suggestions**: Get 3 AI-generated reply options (formal, friendly, short)
- **Templates**: Pre-built response templates for different star ratings
- **Routing**: Assign reviews to team members by location
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Automatic tagging of review sentiment

### Google Business Profile (GBP) Management
- **OAuth Integration**: Secure 2-way sync with Google
- **Profile Updates**: Manage NAP (Name, Address, Phone), hours, categories
- **Photos & Posts**: Upload photos and publish posts to GBP
- **Bulk Operations**: Update multiple locations simultaneously (Pro and above)

### Impact Reports (Monthly Revenue)
FARO's signature feature that proves ROI:

**Basic Tier - Visibility Check (1/month)**
- Visibility score (0-100)
- Review stats and response time
- Basic recommendations

**Plus/Pro Tier - Monthly Revenue Impact Report (4-12/month)**
- Visibility score (78/100)
- Lead breakdown: 47 new leads identified (12 hot, 19 warm, 16 cold)
- Estimated revenue: $3,600 generated this month
- Conversion analysis by channel (GBP: $2,100, WhatsApp: $1,200, Reviews: $300)
- Competitive positioning vs. local competitors
- Top 3 actionable priorities for the week

**Elite Tier - Competitive Intelligence Report (30/month)**
- Full Revenue Impact Report
- Head-to-head competitor analysis
- Market gaps to exploit
- Weekly performance snapshots

### AI Tools & Credits
- **AI Reply Suggestions**: Generates 3 reply options in seconds
- **Review Templates**: 5-star, 3-star, and 1-star specific responses
- **Context-Aware**: Considers conversation history and business type
- **Auto-Drafts**: AI generates campaign copy and social posts (Pro+)
- **Chatbot**: Auto-respond to FAQs (Pro+)

### Automations
- **Tags & Routing**: Automatically tag and route conversations (Plus+)
- **Review Requests**: Automated review request campaigns (Pro+)
- **Follow-ups**: Scheduled follow-up messages (Pro+)
- **Escalations**: Auto-escalate to human when AI confidence is low (Pro+)

### Listings Sync
Synchronize business information across multiple directories:
- **Basic**: 5 directories (Google, Facebook, Apple, core platforms)
- **Plus**: 10 directories (adds Yelp, key vertical sites)
- **Pro**: 20 directories (extended coverage)
- **Elite**: Custom coverage based on agency partnership

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Billing & Pricing

**Q: How does per-location pricing work?**  
A: You pay for each physical location or service area. One restaurant with 3 branches = 3 locations. Your agency sets the final pricing you pay.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my WhatsApp conversation limit?**  
A: Overages are billed through your agency's usage wallet at $0.015/conversation (LATAM) or $0.05/conversation (North America) + platform fee. You'll receive a notification at 80% usage.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade tiers mid-month?**  
A: Yes, through your agency. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades at the next billing cycle. Billing is pro-rated.

**Q: Is there a minimum contract?**  
A: That depends on your agency. FARO offers month-to-month billing to agencies, but your agency may have different terms.

### Features

**Q: Do I get my own branded portal?**  
A: Yes! Starting at the Plus tier, your agency provides a fully white-labeled portal. At Elite tier, this can be on a custom domain (e.g., portal.youragency.com).

**Q: How accurate is the revenue attribution in Impact Reports?**  
A: Revenue estimates are based on your average booking value (set during onboarding) and lead conversion rates tracked through the system. For most businesses, it's 85-90% accurate.

**Q: Can I integrate FARO with my existing CRM or e-commerce platform?**  
A: API access and webhooks are available at the Scale tier (agency level). Your agency can build custom integrations. Native Shopify/WooCommerce integrations are roadmapped for Q3 2026.

**Q: What languages are supported?**  
A: Full UI support for Spanish, Portuguese, and English. AI auto-translation for conversations coming in Q2 2026.

### Support

**Q: How do I get support?**  
A: All support is provided through your agency partner. Higher tiers include priority support and faster SLAs with your agency.

**Q: Can I add more user seats?**  
A: Yes, through your agency. Additional user packs (5-20 users) are available as add-ons.
